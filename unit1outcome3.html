3.2 Describe ways to improve productivity and efficiency
For me personally, I found it really useful to learn keyboard shortcuts as this limits the use of a mouse, which short term isn’t much time wasted, but when you work for hours and hours having to keep switching to your mouse does waste time and not jus that, it’s much more comfortable to be able to utilise the keyboard to it’s full potential. Quite a sad truth, but even a simple case of putting your phone away out of sight or in a place where you won’t hear it or see it is very beneficial. Peoples whole life can be on a phone (emails, work, social networks etc) and putting all that stuff to one side to be able to focus on one thing gives you one less thing to think about so you can put all your effort into what you’re doing.
